### Material Classes (all subclasses of MaterialEntity)
ex:Glass a owl:Class ; rdfs:subClassOf bfo:MaterialEntity .
ex:Sepharose a owl:Class ; rdfs:subClassOf bfo:MaterialEntity .
ex:DEAE a owl:Class ; rdfs:subClassOf bfo:MaterialEntity .

### Artifact Individuals
ex:PackedChromatographyColumn rdf:type core:MaterialArtifact ;
    core:hasComponentPart ex:ChromatographyColumn ,

    core:isMadeOf ex:PortionOfGlass .

    core:isMadeOf ex:PortionOfSepharose ,
                  ex:PortionOfDEAEMolecules .
